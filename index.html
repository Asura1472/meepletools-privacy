Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026

MeepleTools is a board game utility app provided by Decafsoft. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

MeepleTools does not directly collect, store, or transmit personal information. The app does not require user registration, login, or account creation.

2. Local Data

App settings and tool-related data may be stored locally on your device. This data is used only to provide app functionality and is not sent to our server.

3. Advertising

MeepleTools may display advertisements using Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and process certain device information, advertising identifiers, usage data, and other data as described in Google’s own privacy policies.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services provided by Google, including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

5. Children’s Privacy

MeepleTools is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may process data according to their own policies.

7. Security

Since MeepleTools does not directly collect personal information, we do not operate a separate server for storing user data. Locally stored app data remains on the user’s device.
